## DIRECTOR'S NOTES

**Pacing:** Hard cuts throughout — no slow dissolves except the final logo reveal.  
**Color grading:** Deep shadows, neon accents. Gold highlights on every "earn" moment. Celo green on every "confirm" moment.  
**Typography:** The brand font (black weight sans-serif) for all on-screen text. Avoid decorative fonts — power comes from weight and contrast.  
**Sound design:** Real wallet notification sounds (not gimmicky), the on-chain confirmation chime should feel SATISFYING. The streak build-up must feel earned.  
**Accessibility:** Hard-coded subtitles throughout. Every VO line appears as on-screen caption.
